About Us

Adore Technologies is a Bangalore headquartered HR Consultancy firm, primarily engaged in the business of providing professional STAFFING SERVICES to various industries in India.

At Adore Technologies, we focus on reducing overhead cost while delivering best in class solutions. We assist our clients in finding the right people to support their business strategy. Our staffing strategy is customized to suit the needs of each client. Whether it’s staffing a project, an entire department or a single position, we will assist you in filling your positions in a timely and cost effective manner.

Project Staffing

Managing and working on complex, high-stakes projects that have critical deadlines and tight resources requires experienced project management professionals. Our business model allows us to align and approach each project in accordance with client’s requirements.

All of our consultants are required to complete the project training that we provide. These professionals are trained on our standards, methodology and guidelines. We also provide them with insight and detailed information about the client. Our consultants show up prepared to provide instant value and contribute to the success of the project.

We partner with our clients and professionals by providing mutually profitable relationships and being responsive to the needs of all parties. We deliver value by staffing project teams with experts who share our guiding principles of competency, integrity and honesty.
